# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Accounts is used by small-business owners and operators who need to manage company finances without adding unnecessary accounting workflow complexity. They are typically checking cash position, recording income and outgoings, reviewing pending transactions, managing clients, and exporting reports while running the rest of the business.

## Product Purpose

Accounts helps users keep company financial records current and accessible. Success means users can quickly understand their financial position, record or review transactions, switch company context, and reach related account tasks without confusion.

## Brand Personality

Modern and efficient. The product should feel current, practical, and professional while preserving the existing Motech Accounts identity.

## Anti-references

Avoid rebrand moves such as unrelated brand colours, ornamental interface styling, invented affordances, decorative dashboards, or marketing-led composition. Avoid UI that feels clever at the expense of task clarity.

## Design Principles

- Preserve identity while modernising structure, spacing, and interaction patterns.
- Put task clarity ahead of decorative novelty.
- Only expose information, states, and actions supported by the existing GraphQL API or established application workflows.
- Keep company and account context visible without duplicating it.
- Use familiar product affordances for navigation, notifications, account controls, and actions.
- Improve accessibility when the existing identity needs contrast or state clarity adjustments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Preserve the existing visual identity where possible, but adjust colour values, state styling, focus affordances, and text contrast where accessibility requires it.
